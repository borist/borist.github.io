Hi, I'm Boris. I'm currently a student at the University of Pennsylvania, previously a software engineering intern at Google. I am interested in Technology, Finance, and the intersection of the two.
These are some of the projects I've worked on in the past. If you'd like to see a few more, check out my github account.

PennCab

A web app that allows Penn students to sign up to share cab rides with one another. Users can create, manage, and join cab rides that are under capacity and in the future. Created using Python and Django.

Preview of PennCab

Options Graphing

A web app for graphing financial options and their underlying assets. Can be used to construct derivatives using a number of assets and graph their combined payoff. Useful when learning about financial derivatives.

Preview of Financial Options Graphing

Winesquare

A "Foursquare for wine" web application for avid wine drinkers. Users can "check in" when they drink a certian wine, view other users' drink histories, and receive recommendations based on their past check ins. Created with PHP, MySQL, and JQuery.

Preview of WineSquare

EventSnap

An Android application that lets you take a photo of an event poster, and have it automatically added to your phone's calendar using Optical Character Recognition. Created at HackNY Spring Hackathon 2012.

PennMeet

Created to "take the suck out of club list-servs," an Android app that provides cloud-based club management. Users can easily create, add, join, and share groups. Created at PennApps Fall Hackathon 2011.

SafeMode

SafeMode stops you from interacting with specified contacts when you don't want to. It temporarily removes all blacklisted contacts from your phone and blocks outgoing and incoming calls/messages to or from their numbers.
