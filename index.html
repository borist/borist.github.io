PennMeet

Created to "take the suck out of club list-servs," PennMeet provides cloud-based club management. Users can easily create, add, and share groups, either by scanning a unique QR Code or through Bump. Additionally, you can see the profile of any club you join, as well as the user profiles of other people in the club with you!
